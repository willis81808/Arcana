# Arcana
Adds 12 new Legendary cards to the game themed after the Major Arcana of the Tarot.

## Card Stats
- The Devil
- The Sun
- The Hermit
- The Magician
- The Moon
- The Fool
- The Tower
- The Empress
- The Hanged Man
- The Hierophant
- Temperance
- Death
